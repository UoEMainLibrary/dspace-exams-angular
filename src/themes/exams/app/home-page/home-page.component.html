University of Edinburgh Exam Papers Online: 2004/05 to 2023/24

Exam question papers from the 2023-24 diet are now being added here. These pages are updated periodically as papers become available. If you cannot find what you are looking for, try visiting again at a later date or email exam.papers@ed.ac.uk.

Marking Guides and Sample Solutions

Please contact your course organiser or the course secretary to find out if any have been created for your course and how to access them.

Search Tips
  • Typing in the course name or code will bring up suggestions for records already in the database.
  • If you cannot find the paper you are looking for from a title search, try entering the course code. Course codes can change over time. Click the 'All papers for this course' link in your search results to get full results for the course title.
